Hello! I’m Olakunle Owodunni, a dedicated Data Engineer with over a decade of experience in designing, implementing, and optimizing data solutions to drive business intelligence and decision-making. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting from the University of Lagos, and I've worked with reputable organizations including GlaxoSmithKline (GSK), Bway Logistics, GIIT Africa, and Digital Banana, delivering scalable data solutions across industries such as healthcare, logistics, retail, and public administration.

I have also served as a Data Processing Officer at Apapa Local Government for over a decade, where I specialized in data management, revenue analytics, and governance support, ensuring data accuracy for effective policy-making and community development.

With certifications in Microsoft Data Engineering, Data Science, SQL, and Business Intelligence, I am proficient in tools such as Microsoft Azure, Power BI, SQL, Python, and ETL pipelines, using these technologies to build and optimize data architectures. I am also passionate about continuous learning and am currently expanding my expertise in Cloud Computing and Business Intelligence through Coursera.

Beyond my technical career, I am deeply involved in community development, serving as a Youth Leader in the Nigeria Union of Local Government Employees (NULGE) and actively contributing to initiatives at the Ogunmokun Community Development Association. My long-term goal is to leverage data engineering and analytics to enhance governance, optimize public services, and improve the quality of life in communities.

Feel free to explore my projects, work experience, and professional journey. I look forward to connecting and collaborating on impactful data-driven initiatives!
